Juan Lo

About

Juan Lo is a Brooklyn-based DJ known for blending disco, funk, soul, house, and feel-good dance music into sets that feel both nostalgic and fresh. Originally from Detroit and shaped by years in Austin's creative scene, his sound pulls from deep musical roots while staying grounded in community, movement, and energy on the dance floor.

With a background in photography, mixed media art, and nightlife culture, Juan approaches DJing with a strong visual and emotional sensibility. His sets are less about trends and more about creating atmosphere balancing warm grooves, unexpected selections, and high-energy moments that bring people together. Whether playing intimate bars, community events, dance parties, or late-night rooms, he aims to make every set feel personal, vibrant, and alive.

Now based in Brooklyn, Juan Lo is focused on building community through music, art, and culture while continuing to evolve his sound in New York City's nightlife scene.
